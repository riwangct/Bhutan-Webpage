Popular Bhutanese Dishes

Ema Datshi
Spicy chili and cheese stew — Bhutan's national dish.

Kewa Datshi
Potatoes cooked with cheese and green chili.

Shamu Datshi
Mushroom and cheese curry — rich and earthy.

Jasha Maroo
Spicy chicken stew with ginger, garlic, and tomato.

Phaksha Paa
Pork cooked with red chili, radish, and spinach.

Sikam Paa
Dried pork belly with chili flakes — crispy & smoky.

Momos
Steamed dumplings with meat or veggies — street favorite.

Puta
Buckwheat noodles served with spicy sauce.

Hoentay
Spinach and cheese dumplings, especially during festivals.

Goen Hogay
Spicy cucumber salad with chili, onion & coriander.
